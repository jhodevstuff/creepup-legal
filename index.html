Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2024

SUMMARY: CreepUp is a privacy-focused app. All data stays on YOUR device. We do not collect, access, or transmit any of your data. You have complete control over your information.

1. INTRODUCTION

CreepUp ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app collects, uses, and stores data.

IMPORTANT: CreepUp is a privacy-focused app that stores ALL data locally on your device. We do not operate any servers, do not collect any analytics, and do not transmit any data to third parties.

2. DATA COLLECTION

2.1 BLUETOOTH DATA

The app scans for Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) devices in your environment and collects:

  • Device names (as broadcast by the devices)
  • Signal strength (RSSI)
  • Device identifiers
  • Manufacturer information (from public Bluetooth Company ID database)
  • Service information (from public Bluetooth Service UUID database)

This data is collected locally on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

2.2 LOCATION DATA

If you grant location permission, the app collects:

  • GPS coordinates when devices are discovered
  • Location names (via Apple's reverse geocoding)

Location data is:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Used to display devices on the map view
  • Never transmitted to us or any third party
  • Optional - the app functions without location access

2.3 ESP32 HARDWARE DATA

If you connect an ESP32 companion device, the app receives:

  • Bluetooth device information from ESP32
  • WiFi network information from ESP32 (network names, signal strength)
  • ESP32 device status information

This data is:

  • Transmitted only between your ESP32 device and your iPhone via Bluetooth
  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Never sent to any server or third party

3. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

All data is used ONLY on your device for:

  • Displaying discovered devices in the app
  • Triggering proximity alerts
  • Showing devices on the radar visualization
  • Mapping devices on the GPS map
  • Providing device statistics and history

We do NOT:

  • Upload data to any server
  • Share data with third parties
  • Use data for analytics or advertising
  • Access your data in any way

4. DATA STORAGE

All data is stored locally on your device using:

  • CoreData (Apple's local database framework)
  • UserDefaults (for app settings)

Data is stored in your app's sandbox and is:

  • Not accessible to other apps
  • Backed up with your iPhone backup (if enabled)
  • Deleted when you delete the app

5. DATA EXPORT

You can manually export your collected data in CSV format for analysis tools.

When you export data, YOU control where it goes. We do not receive or process exported data.

6. DATA SHARING

We do NOT share any data with third parties because we do not have access to your data.

The only data transmission occurs:

  • Between your iPhone and connected ESP32 device (via local Bluetooth)
  • When you manually export data to apps of your choice

7. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

CreepUp does NOT use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics (no Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services
  • No cloud services

The app uses only Apple's built-in frameworks:

  • CoreBluetooth (for BLE scanning)
  • CoreLocation (for GPS)
  • CoreData (for local storage)
  • MapKit (for maps)

8. PERMISSIONS

8.1 BLUETOOTH PERMISSION

Required for core functionality (BLE device scanning). The app cannot function without this permission.

8.2 LOCATION PERMISSION

Optional - only needed for GPS-based device mapping. You can use the app without granting location access (List and Radar views work without location).

8.3 NOTIFICATION PERMISSION

Optional - only needed for proximity alerts. You can use the app without notifications.

9. DATA RETENTION

Data is retained:

  • Indefinitely on your device (until you delete it)
  • You can delete individual devices or runs from the app
  • All data is deleted when you uninstall the app

10. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

CreepUp is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. SECURITY

Your data security is important to us:

  • All data stays on your device
  • Data is protected by iOS security measures
  • No internet transmission of data
  • No server-side data storage

12. YOUR RIGHTS

You have full control over your data:

  • View all collected data in the app
  • Delete individual entries
  • Delete all data (via app reset)
  • Export data to external tools
  • Revoke permissions at any time

13. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted in the app and on our website. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

14. LEGAL BASIS (GDPR)

For EU users, data processing is based on:

  • Legitimate interest (personal safety and security monitoring)
  • Consent (for optional features like location)
  • All processing occurs locally on your device
